FAQ

What is the meaning of the colours?

Every colors represents the age of the note

Dark Green
Between one second and one day
Green
Between one and 31 days
Lime
Between one and six months
Amber
Between six months and one year
Orange
Between one and two years
Red
Two or more years

NotesReview now has a new, own backend for finding notes!

This allows flexible and noticeable faster queries and easier integration of future changes and additions.
You can find the source code of the new backend here: https://github.com/ENT8R/notesreview-api

Except for faster queries and an improved search, you probably will not notice a difference between the new and the old backend. But this does not mean that there will not be any changes in upcoming versions. Stay tuned for features like regex search and labelling of notes for an even more efficient workflow.

If you spotted a bug or noticed weird and unexpected behaviour, please open a new issue at https://github.com/ENT8R/NotesReview/issues

Thank you for using NotesReview! ❤️
